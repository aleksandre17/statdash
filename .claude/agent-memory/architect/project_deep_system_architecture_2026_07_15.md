Deep system-architecture study (`docs/architecture/audit/DEEP-2026-07-15-system-architecture.md`) — the meta-level truths beneath the settled ADR-041/042 substrate.

**Verdict:** ONE body at framework grade at the SUBSTRATE level (one type system, one containment grammar with residence-on-field, executable arrow — ahead of Builder.io/Grafana/Sanity there). UNDER-factored at the META level; the ADR-041 "under-built root / over-built above" disease has moved UP a level.

**The five durable invisibles (deeper than the lead's product study):**
1. **Parts and Facets are two projection engines that are secretly ONE.** Both emit `PropSchema`, both render through one Inspector; `inspect = projectParts ⊕ projectFacets` is a hand-wired binary fold. `FacetDescriptor{appliesWhen,contract→PropSchema,readPath,order}` is 95% the un-named `Projector` abstraction. The Part port is the same law as a bespoke port.
2. **`PropFieldType` is forking** — primitive VALUES (`string/number/enum-ref`) vs capability-SURFACE embeds (`style/data-pipeline/events/visibility`). The embeds are the Facet set re-appearing as field-types = a capability with two declaration homes; a closed union in `core` = the "new capability = edit the type" anti-pattern in miniature. Fix at n=5, not now.
3. **~20 registries, no symbol-table abstraction.** `makeRegistry` unifies the WRITE side; there is NO unified READ model. `describeApp()` is the unbuilt reflective spine AR-46 (Plugin SDK) + the meta-circular Constructor both need.
4. **Artifact identity forked FIVE ways with no shared spine** — SiteConfigResponse / SiteManifestContract / ViewSnapshot / SnapshotEnvelope+PageDataSnapshot / page_version. `schemaVersion` re-declared 3×; publish FSM only in the DB row. AR-43+AR-47+AR-48 are THREE builds of ONE `Publishable{id,version,lineage,provenance,publishState}` axis. Biggest gap on the axis that IS the product (provenance).
5. **Studio not dogfooded** — rail/dock/top-bar/surfaces are hand-coded React; only CanvasView renders through the node system. The inspector/dock ARE already registry projections (partway self-hosted). Correct cut = fence the shell as hand-coded, project the contract-authoring surfaces.

**Maximal target:** "The Declaration and its Projectors" — `everySurface(decl)=fold(projectorRegistry.applicable(decl))`. The platform wrote this law THREE times (ADR-041 port, ADR-042 Triprojection, Facet registry) without naming it once → the concept keeps forking one level ABOVE wherever it was just unified. That is the real root of the owner's "we start architectures that don't come out" at the ARCHITECTURE (not product) level.

**Power moves (all gated AFTER the lead's W1–W5 product waves — opening any now = the 13th-stratum circle):** PM1 Projector meta-model (north-star, gate on AR-43-inspector/N1/AR-46) · PM2 unify Artifact identity (DESIGN now, build on W5) · PM3 `describeApp()` reflective read-model (fold into W2/W3, low risk) · PM4 PropFieldType de-fork (trigger = 5th rich field) · PM5 fence meta-circular boundary (doctrine now).
