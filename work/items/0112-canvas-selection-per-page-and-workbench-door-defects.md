### S1 ŌĆö canvas selection per page ┬Ę VERDICT: REPRODUCED, RACY (timing-dependent, NOT per-page-deterministic)
Not "GDP works, others don't" ŌĆö it's **"the boot page + whichever page you land on with enough content-size change works; sparse/just-navigated pages frequently render 0 clickable frames."**
